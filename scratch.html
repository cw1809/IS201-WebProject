My Favorite Hobby: Off-roading


  1. Some of the benefits of off-roading include:

    • Owning a car that is frequently complimented
    • Seeing some of the most remote places in the world
    • Disconnecting from technology
    • Getting awesome pictures
    • Getting your adrenaline going
    • Having more cool stories to tell

For most of my life I have been into cars, but only recently have I broken into the off-roading scene. It is easily one of the coolest things that I have ever done. There is nothing quite like pulling up to an obstacle that seems impassable and then climbing right over it. Going up a hill that is so steep that all you can see out the front window is the sky gives you a feeling that is hard to come by anywhere else. Most recently I have done White Rim Road and the Dollhouse in Canyonlands National Park. The Dollhouse is one of the most remote regions in Canyonlands, so it is an awesome experience getting to go where only a couple hundred people go each year. See below for a video about the road to the Dollhouse. If you are looking for a new hobby and you aren't afraid to get a little dirty, I would totally recommend getting into offroading.
